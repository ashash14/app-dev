## OVERVIEW
*When Life Gives You Tangerines* is a sweet and emotional show on Netflix. It takes place in the 1950s on Jeju Island in South Korea. The story is about love, family, dreams, and never giving up. It teaches many life lessons through the lives of two young people, Ae-sun and Gwan-sik.

Ae-sun is a loud and brave girl. She dreams of becoming a famous poet. But because her life is very hard and poor, she wants to marry a rich man from Seoul. She believes that money will help her escape her difficult life. Gwan-sik is a quiet and shy boy. He loves Ae-sun very much, even though she does not notice his feelings at first. He is always kind to her and helps her without asking for anything. Even when she says she wants someone rich, he keeps caring for her. One day, Gwan-sik tells Ae-sun that he loves her. He promises to take care of her and work hard for her. They get married and have three children. Life is not easy. One of their children dies during a strong typhoon. Gwan-sik becomes a fisherman to support the family. He works very hard every day and never complains. Even when he is tired or sad, he never shows it to Ae-sun. He always puts her happiness first. As they grow older, their lives change, but their love stays strong. Even when they face war, pain, and time apart, they never stop loving each other.

When Life Gives You Tangerines is not just a love story. It is also a story about growing up, staying strong, and never giving up on the people you love. It shows that true love is not just about words, but about actions and patience. With the beautiful views of Jeju Island and warm, emotional moments, this show feels like a gentle poem. It reminds us to find beauty in small things and to keep hope in our hearts.
